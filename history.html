Urban Guerrillas — Official Clan History

Founders (The Beginning)

UG Jo Moma — The First Shadow, King of the M203

In the earliest days of online warfare, UG Jo Moma carved his legend in the streets of Delta Force: Black Hawk Down. His mastery of the M203 grenade launcher made cover meaningless and fear unavoidable. Jo Moma didn’t just play the game — he defined it.

UG Worm Pile — The M14 Warlord of the Rice Paddies

Before becoming UG MajBoyington, he was UG Worm Pile — the undisputed #1 Men of Valor player in the world. His arena was the rice paddies, where he called out entire squads with a simple challenge: “Meet me in the rice paddies.”

Operation Flashpoint: Elite Era (2005–2006)

After the founders and early squad were forged, the clan entered its first true tactical crucible: Operation Flashpoint: Elite on the original Xbox.

The Most Dangerous and Brutal Clan on Flashpoint

UG became the most feared clan the Flashpoint community had ever seen. Their coordination, precision, and overwhelming force turned every match into a one‑sided operation.

The Cobra Nightmare — UG Jo Moma & UG Worm Pile

If you saw UG Jo Moma, it was already too late — because he was most likely in the Cobra gunship with his favorite gunner, UG Worm Pile.

Jo Moma flew like a predator. Worm Pile gunned like an executioner. Entire squads vanished in seconds. Vehicles never stood a chance. Bases were erased with terrifying speed.

UG PUFF — The Young Blood Who Turned Warriors Into Mist

UG PUFF became a monster in Gears of War. With his signature shotgun, he obliterated enemies so violently that they turned into mist.
