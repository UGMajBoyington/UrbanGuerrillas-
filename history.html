History of the Urban Guerrillas

Born out of urban maps, tight corridors, and improvised tactics, Urban Guerrillas grew from a small squad into a coordinated clan with its own identity, standards, and battlefield culture.

Founding Era

The clan was founded by UG MajBoyington and fellow core members who wanted more than random matches: they wanted structure, camaraderie, and a recognizable presence in every lobby they entered.

Expansion & Operations

Over time, UG expanded into multiple titles and modes, always keeping the same principles: disciplined squad play, clear communication, and a shared respect for the tag.

Modern Era

Today, the Urban Guerrillas legacy is preserved here: in rosters, imagery, and stories that keep the clan alive for future players who discover the name and wonder who we were.

Urban Guerrillas — History written in concrete and crossfire.
