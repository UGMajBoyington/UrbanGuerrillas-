Urban Guerrillas — Official Clan History

Founders (The Beginning)

UG Jo Moma — The First Shadow, King of the M203 in Black Hawk Down

In the beginning, when online warfare was raw and unforgiving, there was UG Jo Moma, and there was Delta Force: Black Hawk Down. In the alleys of Mogadishu, on the rooftops, in the dust‑choked streets, Jo Moma didn’t just survive — he dominated. His mastery of the M203 grenade launcher made cover meaningless and fear unavoidable. He defined the early UG identity: ruthless precision, explosive battlefield control, fearless pushes, and tactical supremacy.

UG Worm Pile — The M14 Warlord of the Rice Paddies, #1 in the World on Men of Valor

Before he became UG MajBoyington, he was UG Worm Pile — the undisputed #1 Men of Valor player in the world. His arena: the rice paddies. He called out entire squads with one challenge: “Meet me in the rice paddies.” And he destroyed them. With his deadly M14, he ruled the paddies like a warlord — snapping targets instantly, tracking movement through tall grass, punishing flanks, and wiping entire squads. The rice paddies weren’t just a location — they were his throne room.

Operation Flashpoint: Elite Era (2005–2006)

After the founders and early squad were forged, the clan entered its first true tactical crucible: Operation Flashpoint: Elite on the original Xbox. This was the first battlefield that demanded real military thinking — and UG didn’t just adapt, they dominated. They became the most dangerous, brutal, and feared clan the Flashpoint community had ever seen.

The Cobra Nightmare — UG Jo Moma & UG Worm Pile

On Flashpoint, there was one sight that made entire servers panic: the Cobra gunship. If you saw UG Jo Moma, it was already too late — because he wasn’t on the ground. He was in the Cobra. And if Jo Moma was flying, his gunner was always UG Worm Pile. Jo Moma flew like a predator; Worm Pile gunned like an executioner. Entire squads vanished in seconds. Vehicles shredded before they could turn. Bases erased with terrifying efficiency.

Flashpoint players said: “If you see the Cobra, you’re already dead.”
The UG version was worse:
“If you see UG Jo Moma… Worm Pile already has you in his sights.”

Modern Warfare Era (2007–2012)

UG PUFF — The Young Blood Who Turned Warriors Into Mist

When UG PUFF entered Gears of War, he was the youngest Guerrilla — the new blood. But with his shotgun, he became a monster. He moved with supernatural rhythm — sliding, flanking, closing distance — and when he fired, people turned into mist. It became his signature. He carried that brutality into CoD4, World at War, MW2, Battlefield — and later into the stars as CMDR UG PUFF. His legend began in the blood‑soaked battlegrounds of Sera.
